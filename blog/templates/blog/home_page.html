Founder's Note

Bachpan Save The Innocence was founded in January 2020 with a vision to create a world of hope where children will know about their rights and raise their voice against sexual assault, whenever required.
Child Sexual Abuse (CSA) is a pressing human right issue and public health concern in our country and yet, we are unaware of it. There are innumerable reasons that make a child vulnerable to sexual abuse and we are working to eliminate those reasons.
We are on a mission to aware and empower everyone to stand their voice against child sexual assault with the power of education and empowerment. We believe that if children are imparted proper education of personal body safety, they will be more alert and careful. However, children are not the only stakeholders we have, we cover the domain of adults as well through different parenting and child rights advocacy workshops.
Our mission not only includes children from getting victimized but it also aims to prevent them from victimizing others.
I thank every member of Bachpan family for their passion and determination towards the mission.

Sincerely,
Rahul Singla
Founder

Vision

We envision a world of hope where everyone in the nation is sensitized on various aspects of body safety education and child rights. This will ensure that all of us can raise voice against child sexual assault and help, children in need of care and protection.

Mission

  • We are working towards imparting knowledge of safe/unsafe touch, Sexual consent, cyber safety and child rights to children.
  • Mobilizing parents and teachers towards the parenting mechanism. Mobilizing youth through youth sensitization program.
  • Connecting children with counsellors to help them deal with the trauma. Provide a platform where anyone is entitled to share their experience of child sexual abuse.
