Bachpan- Save The Innocence

Overview

Bachpan Save The Innocence is a youth organization started in January, 2020 by Mr. Rahul Singla, with a dream to empower our society to raise their voice against child sexual assault. We aim to work on preventive measures of child sexual assault and encourage everyone to report about child sexual abuse.
The vision is being achieved through different sensitisation workshops and support groups for different stakeholders, considering the age appropriate terminology and content.
